Openclique - Brand Identity

Life is all about new experiences. It’s journey for which we play, explore, and discover. Openclique is a gamified social media app that rewards users for visiting, discovering, and exploring new places around the world. Level up, compete with your friends, and share your journey as you earn points for playing a life full of new experiences.

I have developed a logo and some identity elements to envision the mobile app concept. The flamingo - a quirky migratory bird that travels in large packs - embodies the app’s goals of travel and movement, discovery, and community. The unique appeal of the flamingo will also build multiple creative brand elements further on. Based on initial concepts and analyzing the user target audience, I decided to use a bright contrasting palette, where the colours represent warmth, open-mindedness, and technology while still maintaining a universal identity. This work is still in progress.
