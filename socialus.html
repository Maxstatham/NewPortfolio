Socialus - Packaging Concept

As part of a class assignment to explore packaging design, I created an Instagram VR headset concept, named SOCIALUS, that lets you immerse yourself in the perfectly unrealistic world of Instagram. It filters out all real world problems and lets you live in a perfect virtual world. This tech toyâ€™s function and packaging design aims to emphasize the dangerously negative mental health effects of instagram and social media. I designed this box to appeal to a millennial target audience as this age bracket is most engaged with instagram and, therefore, more prone to the negative effects of social media.

VR and social media is something that, in my opinion, will probably be introduced to the market in the near future. Because of this, I designed the box so that it would look like an actual tech product on shelves, to highlight its relevance and potential dangers. The main part of the box and emphasis of the message is in the warning paragraph on the side panel. Social media has proven to present major mental health problems to adolescence, but is not expressed enough in my opinion. The small subtle warning label explains huge negative side effects of the social media product. The use of its subtleness releases a more powerful emotional effect on the viewer.
